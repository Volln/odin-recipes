Korma Curry

Description

It is a simple delicious Indian recipe that is world renowned its unique taste. It contains spiciness and a great range of taste. It is popular in canteens due to its simpliness to make it

Ingriedients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Sunflower oil
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Greek yogurt
  • tomato paste
  • Korma curry sauce package
  • Cashews

Steps

  1. First you need to put some oil on medium-high heat in a wide pan
  2. Put the onions and sautee them until transparent
  3. In the meantime you can wash and rinse the rice and putting it on a stove
  4. After onions get transparent, put the mixture of ginger and garlic in the pan and stir
  5. Soon, after that, put a diced chicken breast inside the pan
  6. After the chicken gets cooked with a nice golden crust, put the korma curry sauce from the package together with the tomato paste and stir well, don't forget to put the lid
  7. As soon as the liquid reduces and the chicken gets coated and it's to mix in Cashews and greek yougurt for extra texture and flavor
  8. Take the rice from the pot and the curry from the pan and combine the two in a plate

Tada! There's your chicken korma recipe
