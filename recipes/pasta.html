Pasta with pancetta and tomato sauce

Description

It's a very simple recipe that I often do for dinners and doesn't require many ingriedients

Ingriedients

  • Pasta, preferably spaghetti
  • An onion, white kind for softer bites and red for crunchier
  • Pancetta / Bacon that's already diced
  • Parmeggiano
  • Tomato sauce
  • Some olive oil
  • Chili power if you want to add some spice

Steps

  1. First you have to fill a pot with water and put it to boil
  2. Then you have to put a pan with a bit of olive oil on medium to high heat
  3. Once the pan is heated, put pancetta inside and stir once in a while and wait for it to get crispy
  4. Chop and dice an onion and put it in the pan once the pancetta get crispy
  5. As soon as the onion gets transparent, turn down the heat to low and pour the tomato sauce inside and let it reduce and thicken
  6. When the timer goes off for the pasta, take it from the stove and put it through a sieve to remove the excess water
  7. When the pasta is out and the sauce is thickened, combine the two in a bowl plate and add parmeggiano

    8. Tada! There's your quick and simple pasta recipe
